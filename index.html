About This Game

Candy Blitz is a supercharged puzzle game that delivers the sweetness of classic match-3 mechanics with the excitement of real-time gameplay. Designed for casual fun and competitive thrill-seekers alike, Candy Blitz invites you into a colorful world of candy mayhem, lightning combos, and endless rewards. Whether you’re in it for the leaderboard or just to relax and swipe through sweet levels, Candy Blitz is your perfect escape!

How to Play

  • Match 3 or More: Swipe to connect identical candies and clear them from the board.

  • Complete Objectives: Reach target scores, collect special items, or clear all jelly in each level.

  • Trigger Blitz Mode: Make fast combos to activate Blitz Mode and boost your score.

  • Earn Coins & Stars: Unlock new worlds, boosters, and customization options.

  • Use Power-Ups: Deploy candy bombs, striped clears, and sprinkle wheels to crush tough levels.

  • Play Daily Events: Special game modes rotate weekly for bonus rewards.

FAQ

Is Candy Blitz free to play?

Yes! The game is 100% free with optional in-app purchases for boosters and cosmetics.

How do I unlock new levels?

Earn stars and complete objectives to unlock the next region on the world map.

Are there leaderboards? Absolutely!

Compete against players worldwide in timed challenges and weekly rankings.

Do I need to be online?

Nope! You can enjoy most features offline, but you’ll need an internet connection to compete in global events.

How do I earn more boosters?

Complete daily tasks, open reward chests, or purchase bundles with earned coins.
